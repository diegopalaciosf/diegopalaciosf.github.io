I am [Diego Palacios](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Diego-Palacios-13), an assistant professor at the Society and Health Research Center
[CISS](https://ciss.umayor.cl/equipo/diego-palacios-phd) at Universidad Mayor (Chile) since 2021, and a young researcher at the Millennium nucleus
for the evaluation and analysis of Drug Policies [(nDP)](https://www.nucleondp.cl/) and the Millennium nucleus on Sociomedicine
[(SocioMed)](https://www.sociomed.cl/).

My work focuses on applying **social network methods** in **schools, family, and organizational settings**.

I am also a member of the board of the **Chilean Society for Social Network Science (CHISOCNET)**.

My teaching is centered around **research methodology** and **quantitative methods**, with a specialization in **social network analysis**.

## Research areas
Peer relations, adolescence, social network analysis, prosociality, aggression, coauthorship networks, friendships, peer influence, substance use.

## Education
- PhD in Sociology, 2020, University of Groningen, The Netherlands  
- Master of Educational Psychology, 2013, Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile  
- Degree in Sociology, 2010, Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile
